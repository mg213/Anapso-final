Welcome to Anapso

The sustainable, Ancient Grecian-inspired, educational and fun lemonade brand! It's made for kids, by kids.

About Us

We are team Supernovas, a group of kids who understand lemonade! We were brought together by Rocket Club Academy, a robotics, STEM, engineering and entrepreneurship program for kids aged 7-14! We were inspired by the grandeur and depth of Ancient Greek culture and myths, so we created Anapso to embrace it while being the perfect childhood beverage.
Made by kids - made for kids!
